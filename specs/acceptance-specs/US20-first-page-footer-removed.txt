;=============================================
; Assembled quarter lesson first pages have no footer, bilingual mode.
;=============================================
GIVEN a quarter whose lessons were uploaded with the stand-alone template carrying a Creative Commons footer.
WHEN the quarter is assembled in bilingual mode.
THEN no lesson first page in the output shows any footer content.
THEN the CC license text appears only within the TOC section of the book.

;=============================================
; Assembled quarter lesson first pages have no footer, single-language mode.
;=============================================
GIVEN a quarter whose lessons were uploaded with the stand-alone template carrying a Creative Commons footer.
WHEN the quarter is assembled in single-language mode.
THEN no lesson first page in the output shows any footer content.
THEN the CC license text appears only within the TOC section of the book.
