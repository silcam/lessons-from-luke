Feature: Return to the requested page after signing in
  As a signed-out visitor who followed a deep link
  I want to be returned to my intended destination after signing in
  So that I do not have to re-navigate to my original page

  Scenario: User is returned to original deep-link after gate-driven sign-in
    Given a signed-out visitor who opened a gated deep link (e.g. /translate/ABC123)
    And they were redirected to sign in
    When they sign in successfully
    Then they are returned to the originally-requested route including its path parameters

  Scenario: User who signs in directly (no redirect) lands on home page
    Given a user who opens the sign-in page directly (not via a redirect from a gated route)
    When they sign in successfully
    Then they land on the home page

  Scenario: External URL as return destination is ignored
    Given a preserved post-login destination that points to an external or absolute URL
    When sign-in completes
    Then that destination is ignored
    And the user lands on the home page
