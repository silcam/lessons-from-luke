;=============================================
; Single-language lesson-opening spacing matches the quarter master.
;=============================================
GIVEN a single-language quarter assembled from the stand-alone constituent lessons.
WHEN a lesson opening is compared against the curriculum owner's single-language quarter master.
THEN the spacing between the lesson-number graphic, lesson title, and overview header matches the master layout.

;=============================================
; Bilingual lesson-opening spacing matches the quarter master.
;=============================================
GIVEN a bilingual quarter assembled from the stand-alone constituent lessons.
WHEN a lesson opening is compared against the curriculum owner's bilingual quarter master.
THEN the spacing between the lesson-number graphic, lesson title, and overview header matches the master layout.
