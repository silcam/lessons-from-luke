;=============================================
; Creating an invitation auto-emails the link and shows it in the UI.
;=============================================
GIVEN an administrator creates an invitation for a new email.
WHEN the invitation is recorded.
THEN the invitation link is automatically emailed to that address.
THEN the same link is shown in the administrator interface.

;=============================================
; Administrator can resend the email for a pending invitation.
;=============================================
GIVEN a pending invitation.
WHEN the administrator chooses Resend email.
THEN the invitation link is emailed again to the bound address.

;=============================================
; Failed email send does not block invitation creation and is surfaced to the admin.
;=============================================
GIVEN an invitation whose automatic email failed to send.
WHEN the administrator views it.
THEN the invitation still exists.
THEN the link is still available to copy.
THEN the administrator is informed the email could not be sent.

;=============================================
; Resend email action is not available for non-pending invitations.
;=============================================
GIVEN an invitation that is accepted expired or retracted.
WHEN the administrator views it.
THEN the Resend email action is not available.

;=============================================
; Invitation email link works for account creation exactly as before.
;=============================================
GIVEN an invitee receives the invitation email.
WHEN they open the link and complete account creation.
THEN their account is created exactly as it is today.
