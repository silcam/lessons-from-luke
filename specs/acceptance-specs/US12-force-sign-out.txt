;=============================================
; Forcing a sign-out revokes sessions without deactivating the account.
;=============================================
GIVEN a user with an active session.
WHEN an administrator forces a sign-out.
THEN the user's active sessions are revoked immediately.
THEN the account remains Active.

;=============================================
; A force-signed-out user can sign in again.
;=============================================
GIVEN a user who was just force-signed-out.
WHEN they sign in again.
THEN sign-in succeeds because the account was not deactivated.

;=============================================
; Force sign-out requires explicit confirmation.
;=============================================
GIVEN an administrator initiates a force sign-out.
WHEN the action is consequential.
THEN they must pass an explicit confirmation step before it takes effect.
