;=============================================
; A missing or unreadable template asset fails the job with a reason.
;=============================================
GIVEN the quarter styles template asset is missing or unreadable.
WHEN an assembly job runs.
THEN the job ends in the failed state with a human-readable reason.
THEN no document is delivered for download.

;=============================================
; A style-load error mid-assembly fails the job the same way.
;=============================================
GIVEN the style-load step itself errors mid-assembly.
WHEN the job runs.
THEN the job ends in the failed state with a human-readable reason.
THEN the operator never receives a book that skipped the template step.
