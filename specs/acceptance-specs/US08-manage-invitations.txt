;=============================================
; The management screen shows all invitations with full details.
;=============================================
GIVEN several invitations in different states.
WHEN an administrator opens the management screen.
THEN each invitation shows recipient email and granted role and status and creation date and acceptance date for Accepted invitations and the administrator who created it.

;=============================================
; Re-copying a pending invitation link returns the same working link.
;=============================================
GIVEN a Pending invitation.
WHEN an administrator re-copies its link.
THEN the same working link is provided again.

;=============================================
; Retracting a pending invitation immediately kills the link.
;=============================================
GIVEN a Pending invitation.
WHEN an administrator retracts it.
THEN its status becomes Retracted and its link stops working immediately.

;=============================================
; Terminal-state invitations remain listed for history without redemption actions.
;=============================================
GIVEN an Accepted, Expired, or Retracted invitation.
WHEN an administrator views the list.
THEN it remains listed for history and offers no redemption.
