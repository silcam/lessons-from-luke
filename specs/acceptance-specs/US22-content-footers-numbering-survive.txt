;=============================================
; Content-page footers resolve per lesson after full style-family application.
;=============================================
GIVEN an assembled quarter with full style-family application (paragraph, character, page, frame, list).
WHEN content pages of different lessons are inspected.
THEN each content page shows its own lesson's footer fields (quarter, book title, lesson number, lesson name).
THEN page numbers are continuous and correct across the book.

;=============================================
; Table of contents and lesson numbering are unaffected.
;=============================================
GIVEN an assembled quarter with full style-family application.
WHEN the table of contents and lesson-number sequence are inspected.
THEN all lessons are present in the TOC in correct order with correct numbers.
THEN only level-1 headings participate in the outline that drives lesson numbering.
