;=============================================
; Assembly is blocked when a lesson or the Table of Contents is missing.
;=============================================
GIVEN a quarter missing one or more of its 13 lessons or its Table of Contents.
WHEN the operator triggers assembly.
THEN no assembled document is produced.
THEN the operator sees a message identifying which lesson or lessons are missing.

;=============================================
; A mid-assembly generation failure ends in a retryable failed state.
;=============================================
GIVEN all constituent documents exist but one fails to generate during assembly.
WHEN the job runs.
THEN the job ends in a failed state with a human-readable reason identifying the failing lesson where possible.
THEN the job offers no partial file.
THEN the operator can re-trigger the action to retry.
