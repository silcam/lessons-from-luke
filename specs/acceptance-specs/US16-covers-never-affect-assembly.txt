;=============================================
; A quarter with no covers uploaded still reports complete.
;=============================================
GIVEN a quarter with all lessons translated and no covers uploaded.
WHEN completeness is evaluated.
THEN the quarter reports complete.
THEN covers are not treated as missing parts.

;=============================================
; Covers never merge into the assembled quarter output.
;=============================================
GIVEN the same quarter after covers are uploaded and translated.
WHEN the quarter is assembled.
THEN the assembled output is unchanged from the no-covers case.
THEN the assembled output contains no cover content.
